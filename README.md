## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Marketing Funnel & Conversion Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** as part of the **Future Intern Data Science Internship – Task 03**.

The objective is to analyze a real-world bank marketing campaign dataset and understand how customers progress through the marketing funnel—from campaign contact to successful conversion. The dashboard helps identify conversion trends, customer behavior, campaign performance, and opportunities to improve marketing effectiveness.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze the complete marketing conversion funnel.
- Measure customer conversion and drop-off rates.
- Compare campaign performance across different contact methods.
- Identify high-performing customer segments.
- Evaluate conversions by month, occupation, education, and age group.
- Generate actionable business recommendations through data visualization.

# 📊 Business Insights

- Overall campaign conversion rate is **11.27%**, indicating significant opportunities to improve campaign effectiveness.
- **Cellular campaigns** generated substantially more successful conversions than telephone campaigns.
- **May** recorded the highest marketing activity and conversion volume.
- **Administrative, Blue-collar, and Technician** customer segments contributed the largest number of successful conversions.
- Customers with **University Degree** and **High School** education accounted for the highest conversion volumes.
- Most successful conversions occurred within the first **1–3 campaign contacts**, emphasizing the importance of early customer engagement.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in **cellular marketing campaigns**.
- Prioritize marketing efforts during **high-performing campaign months**.
- Design targeted campaigns for **Administrative**, **Blue-collar**, and **Technician** customer segments.
- Personalize offers for customers with **University Degree** and **High School** education.
- Improve lead qualification strategies to reduce the current **88.73% drop-off rate**.
- Focus on optimizing the first few customer interactions instead of increasing repeated follow-up attempts.
